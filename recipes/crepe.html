French Crepes Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 2 tbsp butter, melted

Instructions:

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour, eggs, milk, water, salt, and melted butter. Mix until smooth.
  2. Let the batter sit for at least 30 minutes, or up to 2 hours, to allow it to thicken.
  3. Heat a small non-stick skillet over medium heat.
  4. Pour about 1/4 cup of batter into the skillet, tilting the skillet to evenly distribute the batter.
  5. Cook the crepe for about 2 minutes, until the bottom is golden brown. Flip and cook for an additional 1-2 minutes on the other side.
  6. Repeat with remaining batter, stacking the cooked crepes on a plate as you go.
  7. Serve warm with your favourite fillings such as Nutella, fresh fruits, or savory fillings like cheese and ham.
