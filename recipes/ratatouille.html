French Ratatouille Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 eggplant, diced
  • 1 zucchini, diced
  • 1 yellow squash, diced
  • 2 bell peppers, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 can of diced tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup of olive oil
  • 1 tsp of Herbs de Provence
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pot or Dutch oven over medium heat.
  2. Add the onion, bell peppers, and garlic, and saut√© until softened.
  3. Add the eggplant, zucchini, yellow squash, diced tomatoes, Herbs de Provence, salt, and pepper. Stir to combine.
  4. Reduce heat to low and let simmer for 30 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  5. Serve warm, garnished with fresh basil or parsley if desired.
